About

I'm a Fractional Product Marketing Leader who helps early-stage B2B SaaS founders turn complex products into market stories that drive pipeline.

Over the past 10 years, I've built product marketing functions from the ground up at high-growth companies in technical categories like data analytics and agentic AI, including three unicorns and one exit to Salesforce.

Today, I partner with venture-backed teams to handle the critical 0-to-1 work most founders don't have time for: fixing blurry positioning, building the narrative and strategy behind it, and installing an AI-powered, repeatable system. You get the output of a full marketing hire, including positioning, narrative, and sales enablement, without the six-month search to find one.
